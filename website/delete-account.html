⚠ Account Deletion

Delete Your Servix Account

You can permanently delete your Servix account and all associated data at any time. This action is irreversible — please read below before proceeding.

1 Delete directly from the app
The fastest way to delete your account is from within the Servix app:
  • Open the Servix app and log in
  • Go to Me tab (bottom navigation)
  • Tap Settings
  • Scroll down and tap Delete Account
  • Confirm the deletion when prompted
Your account and all data will be permanently deleted immediately.
2 Request deletion by email
If you no longer have access to the app, send us an email from the address registered to your account and we will delete it within 7 business days.
✉ Email support@servix.world
⚠ What to include in your email: Your full name and the email address registered to your Servix account. We may ask for identity verification before processing the request.
! What happens when you delete your account
Data Action Timeframe
Profile (name, photo, bio) Deleted Immediately
Chat messages Deleted Immediately
Hire requests & history Deleted Immediately
Login credentials Deleted Immediately
Uploaded documents (ID) Deleted Within 30 days
Anonymised transaction logs Retained Up to 1 year (legal)
This action cannot be undone. Once deleted, your account, profile, and all data cannot be recovered. If you sign up again, you will start as a new user.
